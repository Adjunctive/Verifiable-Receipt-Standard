Verifiable Receipt Standard

VRS

Receipts you can verify, keep private, and use long after the purchase — without putting your basket on a public ledger, or pretending a blockchain can settle who owns what.

An open standard. No protocol fee, no token, no rent on the core. Draft

Implementing?

Start with the Overview, build against the Specification and Data Formats, and check your output against the Conformance Vectors. For on-chain components, use the contract ABIs and conformance suite.

Draft community specification — unaudited, not for production with real value. The wire/commitment format is frozen as VRS major version 1.
